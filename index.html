France typically ranks as the world's top tourist destination, attracting millions of visitors annually due to its rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and cuisine. Spain follows closely with its vibrant cities like Barcelona and Madrid, along with its sunny beaches. The United States, especially places like New York City, Florida, and California, also see millions of tourists annually. China has become a growing destination due to its unique historical sites and modern cities.

Quarterly Tourist Arrivals to Malaysia according to Region

Tourist arrivals to Malaysia show strong seasonal variations across regions. Asia consistently contributes the most, with peaks in the first and third quarters driven by regional holidays like Chinese New Year and summer vacations. Europe and Oceania display more pronounced seasonal patterns, with European tourists arriving in higher numbers during the winter months (Q4 and Q1), while Australians tend to visit in Q1 and Q3. Tourist arrivals from the Americas are steady, with slight increases in Q1 due to the colder winter back home. Africa, contributing the least, has no significant quarterly patterns.
